My top 3 movies off all-time.

Mean girls

This time around it seems Fey and co. actually made fetch happen.

The Beekeeper

is a captivating short film that beautifully depicts the symbiotic relationship between a dedicated beekeeper and their buzzing companions. With stunning visuals and a soulful soundtrack, it offers a brief yet profound glimpse into the harmony of rural life and the deep connection between humanity and the natural world.

Destroy All Neighbors

is a gripping short film that delves into the suspenseful realm of neighborhood dynamics. The director skillfully navigates tension and betrayal, delivering unexpected twists that keep viewers on the edge of their seats. This thrilling narrative prompts contemplation about the hidden complexities within seemingly ordinary communities.
